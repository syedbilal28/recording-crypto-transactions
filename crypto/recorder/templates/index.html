What's Enchantiom?

Recording your data while trading in open sea and other platforms is beneficial. and getting responses and insights from them is even more crucial. That's where Encahntiom comes in handy! It's a web app for storing data of NFT purchases and sales while also providing a slick interface for smooth data entry. Perfect for traders using the opensea.io platform and other trading platforms.
